    A Step Towards Digital Pakistan

    Property Verification Services (PVS)

    In pursuit of Prime Minister’s Vision of Digital Pakistan, Capital Development Authority has launched Digital Property Verification Service to facilitate the citizens of Pakistan. Anyone can get the property information online while sitting anywhere in the world.

    The primary notion behind the launch of this service is to facilitate overseas Pakistanis, who are considered, “The supreme fortune of Pakistan”. Property verification while purchasing a property in any housing scheme, vertical or commercial project, has been made possible through ingenious efforts of CDA. This service renders Property Verification Report (PVR) which is publicly available and bring forth the following information:

    • Address (Plot No, Street No, Gali No, Road Name)
    • Housing Scheme Layout Plan (LOP) Approval Status
    • Plot LOP Approval Status
    • Type of Land Use permitted in Approved Layout Plan.
    • Total Area (Sq. Yds) information
    • NOC Status
    • Building Plan Approval Status
    • Building Completion Certificate
    • Competent Authority (for dealing approvals in its jurisdiction area)
